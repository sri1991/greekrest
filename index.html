About Us

Crete, the birth place of Zeus, has many wonders to itself; and a significant one out of them is its food. Hence, when it came to choose the destination of our first ever restaurant away from home in the UK, it was a fairly easy decision. Our endeavour is to create something unique yet traditional, with a touch of contemporary style and aura. The Royals both in India & Britain had had a very close relationship with curry and through years of modification and experimentation British curry has reached new heights of triumph and glory which is second to none.

Our highly skilled chefs have been specially invited from the UK who have decades of experience on them in different styles of curry houses all across the UK. We aim to recreate the same charm over here, embracing the Cretan high standards of gastronomic experience. All of our meat and vegetables have been carefully sourced from the local market and we import the finest spices & basmati rice from the Indian sub-continent to go that extra mile which makes the difference.

We hope you enjoy every bit of your experience with us and promise to deliver a great meal followed by an excellent service every time you walk in to our place to make you feel no less than a royal.
